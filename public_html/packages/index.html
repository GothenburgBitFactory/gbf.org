Packaging

The packaging effort begins with a source tarball release.

Source and binary packages are then built by a distributed team of volunteer package maintainers. The process of building the packages can be complex, and may last several days. Once built, the packages are uploaded and take more time to propagate to the servers and mirrors.

Don't be surprised if it takes a week or more for the packages to appear.
